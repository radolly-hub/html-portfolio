About Me

Monsuru Ridwan Abidemi, popularly known as Radolly , is a passionate and versatile educator, frontend web developer, and dedicated advocate for inclusion. A proud graduate of the University of Ilorin, he holds a second class upper degree in Adult and Primary Education and is further certified with the Nigeria Certificate of Education (NCE) in Social Studies and Intellectual Disabilities a testament to his commitment to reaching every kind of learner. Born into the family of Mr. and Mrs. Monsuru Muftau Ilumomi, Radolly carries a deep sense of purpose in everything he does. His fluency in sign language is not merely a skill but a reflection of his genuine love for the deaf community, as he consistently works to bridge the gap between hearing and non-hearing worlds, championing inclusion at every opportunity.
Beyond the classroom, Radolly is a technology enthusiast who moves confidently in the digital age. He is proficient in frontend web development with solid skills in HTML and CSS, and is actively expanding his expertise in JavaScript. His command of Microsoft tools is matched by his familiarity with cutting-edge AI platforms including ChatGPT, Claude AI, Gemini, and Google AI Studio which he leverages to enhance learning and productivity. Above all, teaching remains his greatest hobby and calling; for Radolly, impacting knowledge is not just a profession it is a way of life.
